gonside

In shadows’ embrace, a heartache resides,
Whispering sorrows, where hope subsides.
Aching echoes of love’s shattered dream,
A tear-stained canvas, a melancholy theme.

Through tearful nights, I seek solace anew,
Yearning for solace, a glimmer so true.
But sadness persists, its grip ever tight,
A haunting reminder of joy’s fleeting light.
